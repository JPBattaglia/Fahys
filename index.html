Our Story

Fahy’s Fog & Barrel is a modern whiskey bar inspired by the grit of San Francisco’s waterfront and the warmth of classic pub culture. We pour craft cocktails, curated whiskey, and a rotating tap list designed for unhurried evenings, late-night conversations, and a seat saved just for you.
